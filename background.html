‘”OUR NAME IS CAMELLO’”

A designer, producer, wholesaler and retailer of home decoration products, home furnitures, gifts, clothes and assessories.

With professional education about museum management, Le Nguyen, the owner of Camello has extensive knowlege about Vietnamese culture and tradition. After graduation in 1996, she worked for Vietnam Ethnology in the frame work of many projects, which aimed at helping ethnic minority groups to get higher income through developing and promoting merchandises using ethnic art and traditional craft. Apart from that, traditional craft products of other urban villages were also brought to the market through 1 shops in Hanoi and 1 in Hochiminh city. Camello has been since also wholesaler of interior and exterior decoration products and furnitures to many shops in Hanoi.

Summer 2002, a fashion branch was set up with a fashion shop and a workshop. Camello fashion offers complicated and sublte hand emboidery fashion products and quickly became a hit in Hanoi. Camello workshop has also delivered large commission textile contracts as well as designed and produced uniforms for big companies in Hanoi and neighbouring provinces.

Until now, we have created a large network of providers and customers. Our providers can be from old poor ethnic women or poor ethnic families in mountainous area to famous artists and artisans. Our customers are retail shops in Hanoi and Hochiminh city, large companies and foreign importers. Being a link in this market chain, Camello is proud that we have helped many poor communities to improve their income. To our customers, we are also always confident that we provide higher quality products which were made with better designs, more attention paid in the production and better quality control.
